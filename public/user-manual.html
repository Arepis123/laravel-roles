How to Make a Booking

1Access the Booking System

  • Use the direct link: https://e-booking.e-clab.online
  • Log in with your credentials
  • Navigate to "Booking" → "Create New Book"
  • Choose booking type 'Meeting Room'

2Choose Meeting Room

  • Browse available rooms under "Location"
  • Specify number of attendees under 'Capacity'

3Select Date and Time

  • Choose your preferred date from the calendar
  • Select start and end times
  • Check real-time availability
  • Only available times will be displayed in the dropdown menu

4Fill Booking Details

  • Enter meeting purpose or description
  • Specify number of attendees
  • List any refreshment needs
  • Tick the Technical Support checkbox if needed

💡 Pro Tips for Meeting Room Booking:

  • Book popular rooms (boardroom) well in advance
  • Cancel unused bookings to free up space for others
  • Check room capacity - book 20% larger than your group size
  • Test AV equipment 15 minutes before important meetings
  • Clean up after use - next meeting starts promptly
  • Please be sure to turn off the Smart Monitor after use

1Check Eligibility

  • Verify you have a valid driver's license
  • Ensure your application is validated by your superior before booking
  • Confirm your insurance coverage is up to date

2Access Vehicle Booking Portal

  • Use the direct link: https://e-booking.e-clab.online
  • Log in with your credentials
  • Navigate to "Booking" → "Create New Book"
  • Choose booking type 'Vehicle'

3Select Vehicle Type

  • CRV: Manager Level
  • Aruz: Executive Level
  • Bezza: Non-executive

4Enter Capacity and Destination

  • Specify number of passenger and driver under 'Capacity' field
  • Fill in the distination
  • For a capacity of more than one person, select each passenger's name from the list

5Select Date and Time

  • Choose your preferred date from the calendar
  • Select start and end times
  • For multi-day booking, enter end date
  • Check real-time availability
  • Only available times will be displayed in the dropdown menu

6Booking Details

  • Enter purpose of trip

7Submit and Approval

  • Submit request with business justification
  • Wait for Admin approval
  • Receive confirmation email with vehicle details
  • Collect all the required items, including keys and cards, from the Admin
Vehicle Policy: Personal use is prohibited. Return with same fuel level or more and report any issues immediately.

🚗 Vehicle Booking Best Practices:

  • Book vehicles as early as possible
  • Inspect vehicle before departure and document issues
  • Keep all receipts for fuel and parking
  • Return on time

  • Tablets: Demonstrations, mobile presentations

  • Log in with your credentials

  • Consider compatibility with your existing setup

Troubleshooting & FAQ

Common Issues and Solutions

Q: I can't log into my account
A: Try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" link. Check that caps lock is off and you're using the correct email address Your account must be active to log in.
Q: My booking isn't showing up
A: Refresh the page and check your email for a confirmation. If the issue persists, check "My Bookings" or contact support.
